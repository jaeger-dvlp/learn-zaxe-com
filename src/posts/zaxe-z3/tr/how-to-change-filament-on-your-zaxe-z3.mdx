When you are using your 3D printer, you will eventually have to change the filament in your device. This process is extremely easy and can be done even by people who are using the 3D printer for the first time with relative ease. 

First of all, if you already have a filament installed, you will have to unload it.

<strong className="list-number">2.</strong> Press on **Filament Settings**, 

<strong className="list-number">3.</strong> Choose **Unload Filament**, 

<strong className="list-number">4.</strong> Wait for your extruder to get hot,

<strong className="list-number">5.</strong> When the heating up process is over, pull the filament out of the print head.
